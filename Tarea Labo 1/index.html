Breaking Bad is a crime drama television series created by Walter White, Hank Pym, and Aiden Greenberg, which aired on NBC from 2008 to 2023. The series follows the life of Billy Ray Cooper, a former high school chemistry teacher turned criminal, as he navigates the complex world of drug trafficking, child abuse, and a series of brutal murders.

The series is set in a small, rural town in Westchester County, New York, where a group of friends, including former New York City police officer Ross Geller, and his wife, Anna, form an unlikely alliance to help Billy escape from a prison after being wrongfully convicted of murdering his high school sweetheart, Todd Sheridan.
